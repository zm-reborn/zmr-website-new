Zombie Master
Reborn
Your favorite FPS/RTS hybrid zombie shooter!

Zombie Master: Reborn combines the FPS and RTS experience into one terrifying package. Each round a player is chosen to be the Zombie Master. The ZM's job is to summon and command zombies, and kill the survivors. The unlucky who didn't get picked, assume the role of a survivor. Their goal is to stay alive and complete the map objectives to survive another day!
